# Social Planner — Quick Start

Welcome! Here's how to get planning in about two minutes.

## 1. Open the app
The app opens straight to your Dashboard. If you're browsing before setting
up, you'll see sample content so you can explore every screen — nothing you
do to the sample is saved.

## 2. Activate with your purchase code
Settings → enter the access code from your Etsy purchase. This switches the
app to your own (fresh) planner.

## 3. Connect your Google Sheet (recommended)
Settings → Connect Google. The app creates a spreadsheet called
**"Social Planner Data (app-managed)"** in *your* Google Drive — that sheet is
your database. Your content never touches our servers (there are none).
Connect the same sheet on your phone and computer and they stay in sync.

## 4. Plan your first post
Scheduler → the + button. Give it an idea, a date, a pillar (topic), a format,
and the platforms it should go to. Write the hook, caption and CTA when you're
ready; pick a hashtag group and the tags fill in automatically.

## 5. See it everywhere
- **Calendar** shows your 6-week plan with today highlighted.
- **Monthly Plan** maps the whole month visually.
- **Feed Preview** shows how your grid will look per platform, before you post.
- When it's time to post, tap the copy button on any post: hook + caption +
  CTA + hashtags land on your clipboard as one clean text.

## 6. Track your growth
Performance → log each platform's followers, engagement and reach once a
month. Set goals and watch the rings fill.

## Tips
- **Install it like an app:** look for the install icon in your browser's
  address bar (desktop) or "Add to Home Screen" (phone).
- **Works offline:** everything keeps working without internet and syncs when
  you're back online.
- **Highlight dates:** add Pay Day, launches or travel days in the Calendar so
  your plan works around real life.
