Social Planner

by ArtivicoLab

A social media content planner that lives in your own Google Sheet.

Social Planner is a phone-first web app for creators and small business owners. Plan every post once and see it everywhere: your content calendar, monthly plan, and a per-platform feed preview. It opens in any browser on your phone, tablet, or computer, works offline, and can sync your data to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive so you always own a backup.

Open Social Planner

What you can do

  • Schedule posts with pillar, format, goal, status, hook, caption, CTA, and hashtags
  • Assign each post to up to 8 platforms and preview each platform's feed before you post
  • See a 6-week content calendar and a visual month-by-month plan
  • Save hashtag groups once and auto-fill them on any post, then copy everything in one tap
  • Bank content ideas and track followers, engagement, and reach against monthly goals

How Social Planner uses your Google account

Connecting Google is optional. When you choose to connect, Social Planner requests only the Google Drive "drive.file" permission. This limits access to the single spreadsheet the app creates for you, and nothing else in your Drive.

We use that access for one purpose: to save and sync your planner to that spreadsheet so it is backed up and available across your devices. Your sign-in stays in your browser and talks to Google directly from your device. ArtivicoLab operates no server and never receives, stores, or sells your data. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Your data stays yours

Everything works offline on your device first. If you connect Google, your data lives in a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive that you can open, export, or delete any time. There is no subscription, and connecting to Google Sheets is free.
